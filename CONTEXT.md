# Negestat

Negestat is a marketplace where designers upload designs for print-on-demand merchandise (starting with t-shirts), and customers browse, follow designers/brands, and order products.

## Language

**Order**:
A customer's commitment to purchase a printed product, which the platform guarantees to fulfill regardless of how many other customers order the same design.
_Avoid_: Purchase, transaction

**Print Batch**:
A group of Orders for the same design that are printed together, once the design reaches 10 ordered units (across any size/color variant and possibly multiple units per Order) or its 14-day time-box expires, whichever comes first.
_Avoid_: Run, print job

**Rush Order**:
An Order placed with an extra fee that triggers an individual print job immediately, bypassing the Print Batch threshold and time-box entirely.
_Avoid_: Express order, fast delivery order

**Payout**:
The biweekly disbursement of a designer's accumulated Balance to their payment method.
_Avoid_: Payment, transfer

**Balance**:
A designer's running total of earnings, accrued each time a Print Batch closes or a Rush Order ships, awaiting the next Payout.
_Avoid_: Earnings, wallet
