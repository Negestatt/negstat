# User story

#### Default account
As a customer, I want to browse trending designs, search for creators / brands I care about. And buy the product I want. I would also like to get notifications for new design drops.

#### Designer Account
As a designer my goal is to get my designs on t-shirts. I would like to create a mockup and post them so people can order it. I would like to see my products, and the stats of my sales. And manage my funding methods to get payments.

*The designer account is a super-set of a default account.*

# Business Logic
Once a designer posts their design, they will be promoted by default, and once there is a minimum order of 10 then the design is shipped to print.

If a designer uses copyrighted content, they will need to first get the consent of the copyright holder to then get payment.
